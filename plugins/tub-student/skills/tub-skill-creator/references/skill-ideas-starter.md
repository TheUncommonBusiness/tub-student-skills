# Skill Ideas by Business Function

Not sure what to build? Here are proven skill ideas organized by what they do for your business. Each one solves a real, repeated problem — the kind of thing you'd do more than twice.

---

## Content & Marketing

**Brand Voice Builder** — Walks you through defining your brand's tone, vocabulary, and style. You answer questions about your business and audience; it produces a voice guide you can use everywhere.
*Output: A reusable voice guide document*

**Content Calendar Generator** — Takes your goals, audience, and platforms and builds a month of content ideas with hooks, formats, and posting schedule.
*Output: A structured monthly calendar with topic ideas*

**Social Media Hook Writer** — Given your topic and audience, generates scroll-stopping opening lines for posts, reels, and ads. Tests hooks against proven engagement patterns.
*Output: 10 hooks scored by engagement potential*

**Email Sequence Builder** — Builds a multi-email sequence (welcome, nurture, sales, re-engagement) based on your offer and audience. Each email has subject line, body, and CTA.
*Output: Ready-to-send email sequence*

**Blog Post Outliner** — Turns a topic into a structured outline with SEO-friendly headers, key points per section, and internal linking suggestions.
*Output: Detailed blog outline ready to write*

---

## Sales & Revenue

**Sales Call Prep** — Before a call, give it the prospect's info and it builds a prep sheet: their likely pain points, questions to ask, objections to expect, and your best talking points.
*Output: A one-page call prep sheet*

**Proposal Builder** — Takes your service details and the prospect's situation and generates a professional proposal with scope, deliverables, timeline, pricing, and next steps.
*Output: A complete client proposal*

**Pricing Strategy Advisor** — Walks you through pricing decisions using proven frameworks. Asks about your costs, competition, value delivered, and willingness to pay.
*Output: Pricing recommendation with rationale*

**Objection Handler** — Collects your most common sales objections and builds responses for each one. Covers price, timing, competition, and "I need to think about it."
*Output: An objection response playbook*

---

## Operations & Systems

**SOP Builder** — Turns a process you do repeatedly into a step-by-step SOP anyone on your team can follow. Asks you to walk through the process, then documents it clearly.
*Output: A formatted standard operating procedure*

**Meeting Agenda Generator** — Given the meeting purpose and attendees, creates a structured agenda with time blocks, discussion topics, decisions needed, and action items template.
*Output: A ready-to-share meeting agenda*

**Project Delegation Tool** — Takes a task you want to hand off and creates a delegation brief: objective, scope, deliverables, timeline, resources, and success criteria.
*Output: A clear delegation brief*

**Weekly Report Automator** — Pulls together your key metrics, wins, blockers, and priorities into a formatted weekly report. Asks what matters most to your audience.
*Output: A professional weekly status report*

---

## Customer Success

**Client Onboarding Flow** — Builds a step-by-step onboarding sequence for new clients: welcome messages, setup tasks, check-in schedule, and milestone markers.
*Output: A complete onboarding plan*

**Feedback Analyzer** — Takes survey responses, reviews, or feedback data and extracts themes, sentiment, and actionable insights. Prioritizes what to fix first.
*Output: A structured feedback analysis with priorities*

**Retention Check-In Drafter** — Creates personalized check-in messages for clients at key moments (30 days, 90 days, renewal). Warm, specific, and action-oriented.
*Output: Ready-to-send check-in messages*

---

## Picking Your First Skill

Ask yourself:
1. **What do I do more than twice a week?** That's your highest-ROI skill.
2. **What takes me the longest?** Even saving 20 minutes per use adds up fast.
3. **What would help my team if I'm not available?** Skills that capture your expertise are force multipliers.

Start with ONE skill. Get it working. Then build the next one.
